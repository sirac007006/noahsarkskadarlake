About Noah's Ark Skadar Lake

Noah’s Ark Skadar Lake Journey

Experience Skadar Lake in an unforgettable way. Nestled in the picturesque regions of Montenegro, Skadar Lake is a true wonder for nature lovers, history enthusiasts, and those seeking a peaceful oasis.
As the largest lake in the Balkans, it boasts pristine beauty, diverse wildlife, and charming fishing villages.
Our guided tours offer the chance to immerse yourself in the unique charm of Montenegro, with Skadar Lake as the perfect backdrop.
We offer exclusive boat rides on our fleet of three handcrafted wooden boats, blending tradition and comfort to provide a truly authentic experience. The boats can accommodate up to 50 passengers, making them ideal for group tours, family outings, and corporate gatherings.

What to Expect?

Flexible tour durations: Choose an excursion lasting from 1 to 5 hours, exploring serene waters and stunning landscapes at your own pace.

Nature and Wildlife

Observe rare birds, glide through shallow channels adorned with lotus flowers, and enjoy the untouched natural beauty of the lake.

Customized Excursions

Personalized tours for history lovers, enthusiasts, or those seeking relaxation in nature.
